Our Story

Rooted in Quality,
Grown with Care

For decades, Locust Grove Nursery has been a trusted name among landscapers, garden centers, and municipalities across northwestern Pennsylvania. From our greenhouses along Old State Road in Waterford, we grow and supply a diverse selection of premium nursery stock, ornamental trees, and flowering plants.

As a wholesale grower, our focus is on providing the highest quality plant material with the personal service that only a family-run operation can deliver. When you work with us, you're working directly with the people who grow your plants.

"We don't just sell plants — we grow relationships. Every order gets the same attention whether it's ten flats or ten pallets."

What We Grow

From hardy native species to the latest Proven Winners selections, we grow a wide range of plant material to meet the needs of landscape professionals and retail garden centers.

Ornamental Trees & Shrubs

Shade trees, flowering ornamentals, and evergreen specimens grown for durability and visual impact in any landscape.

Perennials & Annuals

Season-long color from spring ephemerals to fall favorites. Grown on-site and hardened off for the region's climate.

Proven Winners Selections

Authorized grower of Proven Winners varieties including Buddleia, hydrangeas, and other top-performing cultivars.

Custom Growing Programs

Need specific varieties or volumes for your operation? We offer contract growing tailored to your requirements and timeline.
